Quality, Environment and Safety

It is G.D.'s primary commitment to provide highly competitive products and services that fully satisfy the explicit and implicit needs of customers. The company's internal and external resources are committed to achieving the objectives of Quality and Efficiency in all business processes while ensuring compliance with the principles for the protection of health, safety at work, and the environment.

Certifications

G.D has obtained the UNI EN ISO 9001 certification since 1998. Over time, the Quality System has evolved in compliance with the periodic revision of the reference legislation. In 2020, G.D obtained the extension of ISO 9001 for the production of machinery and complete lines for the pharma sector.

In 2008, G.D's environmental management was certified according to UNI EN ISO 14001:2015, ensuring compliance with environmental impacts and legislation.

In 2020, G.D completed the migration from the OHSAS 18001:2007 standard to the new UNI ISO 45001:2018 standard for health and safety at work, allowing for continuous improvement of performance.
