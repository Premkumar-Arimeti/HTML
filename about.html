G.D. is a leader in machines for the production and packaging of cigarettes, filters, other tobacco products, and special products. Thanks to its wide range of machines, G.D is the only supplier of complete high-speed lines with a variety of solutions for the tobacco industry.

With its worldwide network of branches and service centers, G.D is always close to its customers, wherever they are. Providing added value to its customers is a vital commitment for G.D., which benefits from synergy with the companies of the Coesia Group to create comprehensive support for customers to enhance their products.

Matteo Lena is the CEO of G.D.
